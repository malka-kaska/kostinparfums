# Stories Behind the First KOSTIN Campaign
Date: 2026-07-06

Originalni parfiumi ot nai-prestijnite svetovni marki. Za KOSTIN produktoviat selektiran na mestoto e samo nachalo. Izbrahme 12 arhivni aroma s visoka dobavena stoynost: niche oud, floral luxury, iconic gifts and modern designer staples. S tova izdanie zapochvame strategiyata s avtentichnost, baza dostavka i edna istorisya, koqtmo mozhe da se oblike.

## Campaign directions
1. Cold prospecting - Advantage+ catalog.
2. 24h retargeting.
3. Cart rescue.
4. Past-buyer upsell.
5. Editorial carousel A/B.

## Goals
- CTR >= 1.2%
- CPM <= EUR 9
- ATC rate >= 9%
- CPA <= EUR 28
